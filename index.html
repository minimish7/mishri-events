About Mishri

Sweet by name. Known for the moments that linger.

Mishri — the Hindi word for "sweet" — is both a name and a promise. Every event we touch is infused with the warmth of genuine care, cultural understanding, and a meticulous attention to detail that only comes from truly loving what you do.

"The best events don't just look beautiful — they feel like the people in them."

Mishri Events has spent nearly a decade bringing this philosophy to weddings across the Pacific Northwest and beyond — from grand South Asian celebrations of 300+ guests to intimate gatherings of twenty. We've also produced corporate summits at Google where human connection matters as much as flawless logistics. Whatever the scale, the standard of care is the same.

Manisha's relationship with celebrations runs deeper than planning — she has served as emcee at close family and friend weddings, standing at the center of some of the most sacred moments in people's lives. That warmth doesn't leave you when you step behind the scenes. It's in everything we do.

Our Services

Every detail, thoughtfully held

Whether you need someone to carry the full weight of planning or simply to step in on the day you've spent months preparing for — we show up for you, completely.

From the first spark of an idea to the final moment of your event — we carry the full journey with you. Venue, vendors, design, timeline, and day-of execution, all handled with care.

Our Values

Community is at the heart of everything we do

We don't believe in cookie-cutter celebrations. We believe in the specific, unrepeatable magic of your people in a room together — whether that's 20 or 400. That's what we build toward — every time.

Warmth at Any Scale
Whether you're gathering 20 people or 400, every guest deserves to feel seen. We bring the same personal care to a grand South Asian wedding as we do to an intimate dinner celebration.
Purposeful Design
Nothing is an afterthought. Every detail is made with purpose — rooted in your story, your culture, and the specific people you're gathering.
Genuine Partnership
We're not just planners — we're collaborators. We listen deeply, ask better questions, and show up as a trusted partner from first call to last dance.
Cultural Fluency
We honor the rituals and traditions that make your celebration uniquely yours — weaving them into every element with respect and creativity.

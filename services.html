Weddings

South Asian weddings are not a single day.
We plan for all of them.

From mehndi nights and haldi ceremonies to baraats and welcome nights, we understand the multi-day, multi-event nature of desi celebrations. We bring structure, warmth, and cultural fluency to every moment.

Full-Service Planning
We carry the full journey with you — from your first vision board to the final dance. Venue selection, vendor sourcing, timeline build, and complete day-of execution.
Day-of Coordination
You did the dreaming. We bring it to life. We manage the run-of-show so you can be fully present with the people you love — from mehndi night through the reception.
Wedding Emcee
Manisha has emceed welcome nights and receptions for close family and friends — bringing natural warmth, crowd energy, and thoughtful games that bring guests together.
Vendor Sourcing
Access our handpicked network of photographers, florists, caterers, and more — vendors we trust because we've seen their work up close.
Multi-Event Scheduling
We build detailed timelines for every event in your wedding weekend — mehndi, haldi, baraat, reception — and coordinate logistics across family, vendors, and venues.
Family Task Coordination
We organize tasks for your family so everyone knows exactly what to do and when — from hair and makeup logistics to photo preparation — so nothing falls through the cracks.
